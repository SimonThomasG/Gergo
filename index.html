This program was created to help anyone who is struggling with lower-back problem or wants to prevent it. Due tot he amount of sitting all we know lower-back pain is extremly common. The program I offer is a carefully selected set of exercieses that threat and at the same time prevent lower-back pain. Includes mobilization, SMR and stretching exercieses. These exercieses have a good effect ont he body individually but when they used together they amplify each other’s effect. Regardless of age and physical condition, anyone can do the exercieses, except for those whose with severe spinal problems or hernia. The exercieses are guided on different levels from non-sportman to sportman.

Methods That Work

Mobilization

Mobilization is a restoration and prevention of the range of motion of the joints. We move the joints through the entire range of motion with simple load-free exercieses that can be performed by everyone. The restored joints movement has a positive effect on the surrounding joints and muscles as well, thereby helping to perform subsequent exercieses.

SMR – Self Myofascial Release

Using a Self Myofascial Roller or Foam Roller has a beneficial effect ont he myofascial system. With its use, the range of motion of the entire body increases and pain is alleviated. It relaxes tight muscles and helps to release the tension in them. Professionally guided exercieses during the sessions help you to learn the correct use for the best result.

Stretching

Stretching helps maintain muscles and prevent injuries. Flexible muscles make movement easier, no matter what kind of movement is it. Like mobilization and foam rolling, stretching adds to the body’s range of motion. During the sessions we perform static stretching targeted at those areas that relieve tension around the lower back.
